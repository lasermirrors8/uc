The fight reaches its crescendo in the third when Saint Denis traps Pimblett in a crucifix position, unloading a series of unanswered strikes until the referee intervenes at 3:12 of the final round.

III. WHY THIS BATTLE MATTERS: This bout transcends the rankings; it is a clash of identities. It pits the modern, internet-era influencer fighter against the archetypal warrior who treats every cage walk like a mission objective. This is not about belts or bonuses; it is about proving which path—the charismatic showman or the cold-blooded operative—reigns supreme in the brutal crucible of the Lightweight division.
